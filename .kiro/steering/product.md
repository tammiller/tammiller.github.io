# Product Overview

Ladybugs Aachen is a community website for women in software engineering in Aachen, Germany. The site serves as the digital presence for a local tech meetup group that organizes workshops, talks, and networking events.

## Purpose
- Promote and showcase the Ladybugs Aachen community
- Provide information about upcoming events and workshops
- Create a welcoming space for women in tech to connect
- Facilitate event registration and community engagement

## Target Audience
- Women working in software development in Aachen
- Tech professionals interested in diversity and inclusion
- Local tech community members seeking networking opportunities

## Key Features
- Event listings with structured data for SEO
- Bilingual content (German/English)
- Call for speakers section
- Community information and contact details
- Mobile-responsive design
- SEO-optimized content

## Brand Identity
- Friendly, welcoming, and professional tone
- Focus on creating a "safe space" for learning and networking
- Emphasis on technical content balanced with soft skills
- Community-driven and inclusive approach
